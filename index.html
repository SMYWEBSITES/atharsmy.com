About

  • I am referred as a walking encyclopedia of Insurance information by my colleagues. I currently hold the position of Principal Officer - Basket Option Insurance Brokers Pvt Ltd. Along with a PGDMBA, I have done Associate from the Insurance Institute of India. I have around 30 years of experience out of which a good 21 years has been in the insurance industry, having worked with companies like Max Life Insurance, New India Assurance, Bajaj Allianz, HDFC Standard Life etc. I have a penchant for details and an uncanny knack and can keep my customers comfortable. I am excellent when it comes to PR work. I am strong believer of the philosophy, that "learning is a lifelong process", I am always in the mode of updation of new developments in the insurance sphere. I have done a Specialized Diploma in Health Insurance as well. As man of ethics, I strictly go by the rule book, and always educate the clients on the product, to ensure they enjoy the right benefits.
